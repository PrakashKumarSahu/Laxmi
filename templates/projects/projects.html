Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide complete project kits?

Yes, we offer complete project kits with all necessary components, instructions, and sometimes even pre-written code for common projects.

Can I get guidance for my college project?

Absolutely! Our technicians have experience helping students with academic projects and can provide guidance on circuit design, component selection, and troubleshooting.

Do you offer discounts for students?

Yes, we offer special student pricing on components and project kits. Just show your student ID to avail the discount.

What if I need a component that's not in stock?

We can usually source specialized components within a few days. Just let us know what you need, and we'll check availability.
