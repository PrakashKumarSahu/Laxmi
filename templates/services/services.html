Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the complexity of the issue and parts availability.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes, we offer a 30-day warranty on all our repair services for the same issue.

Can you repair devices at my home?

For most repairs, we need to bring the device to our workshop. However, we offer pickup and delivery services for larger items.

What if my device can't be repaired?

If we determine that repair is not feasible, we'll inform you before proceeding and there will be no charge for the diagnosis.

Do you use genuine parts?

We use high-quality parts from reputable suppliers. For critical components, we can source OEM parts if requested.

What are your service hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday, 10 AM to 8 PM. You can bring devices for repair during these hours.
