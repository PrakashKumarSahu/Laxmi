About Laxmi Electronics

Your trusted electronics partner in Raipur since 2010

Our Story

Laxmi Electronics and Electricals was established in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable electronics repair and products to the community of Raipur. What started as a small repair shop near Durga Chowk has grown into a trusted destination for all electronics needs.

Founded by Mr. Rajesh Sharma, an electronics engineer with over 15 years of experience, our shop has built a reputation for quality service and honest pricing. We take pride in helping our customers extend the life of their electronics through expert repairs while also providing new and refurbished products at accessible prices.

Over the years, we've particularly enjoyed supporting students with their electronics projects, providing both components and guidance to help the next generation of engineers and innovators.
